Amazing Cat Facts

Did you know these fascinating facts about our feline friends?

1

Purring Power

A cat's purr vibrates between 20-50 Hz, which can help heal bones and reduce pain in humans.

2

Sleep Champions

Cats sleep 12-16 hours per day, spending 70% of their lives sleeping or dozing.

3

Super Hearing

Cats can hear sounds up to 64,000 Hz, while humans can only hear up to 20,000 Hz.

4

Ancient Companions

Cats have been domesticated for over 9,000 years and were worshipped in ancient Egypt.

5

Unique Nose Prints

Just like human fingerprints, every cat has a unique nose print that can identify them.

6

Athletic Abilities

Cats can jump up to 6 times their length and can run up to 30 mph in short bursts.
